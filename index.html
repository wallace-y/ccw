Collymoon Craft Gin

In the mediaeval village at the head of a broad, lazy sea-reach in Loch Lomond where locals grow cornmeal, malt and potatoes, we carefully craft our Collymoon as a celebration of our unique landscape and strong cultural heritage. We bring together heritage barley from Brucefield Farm with soft peaty springs which trickle down from the loch side through our fields to lend its ancient earthiness. Salt water drawn up by aqueducts from deep in Loch Lomond sizzles through walls of old steel stills to create perfect clarity and diffusive flavours suitable for mixing Cocktails.
