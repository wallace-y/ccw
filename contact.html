From humble beginnings

Collymoon Craft Gin is a Scottish product that is an excellent representation of classic Highland-style gin. This product has a small, local operation and it relies on locally available farms when creating this outstanding drink. The owners carefully select the botanicals used and give them plenty of time to ferment before distilling. This creates an uncommon taste of varying depth and flavor that makes it a standout in the global market.

The founder

Nicola is the creator of Collymoon Craft Gin and was inspired to create it because of the beauty in nature all around her. The gin is considered a fine example of Scottish craftsmanship and it is made with some refreshingly unusual flavors like rhubarb, mangosteen, osmanthus blossoms, long pepper, ginger and lemon myrtle among others.
